Austin Tenants' Council Website Revamp

As part of the Knowbility OpenAir Competition, I worked with three other students to recreate Austin Tenants' Council website. We created a completely new website for them and worked with them to organize their information and implement accessibility features on the site. Because they requested a content management system, we used SquareSpace as the basis for the site and added on customized HTML and CSS ourselves. Take a look at the site we built!
