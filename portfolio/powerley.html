Powerley Project

This project was done for my analytics capstone project. I worked with three other students and an energy management platform. Our research project consisted of the visualization and analysis of home energy usages for the clients of a power management utility company. This was an exploratory analysis to see if trends can be observed to better advise homeowners in terms of efficient energy consumption. Our analysis was based in the coding language Python with usage of data visualization and analytics tools as well as the interactive analytics software Tableau. Our analysis shed light on temporal and external trends in energy usages such as higher consumption in hotter times of the year, higher usage in mornings and early evenings as well as the behavior of individual, within-home usage patterns.
